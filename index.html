Subway tips
  • Tap a contactless card (OMNY) — no MetroCard needed
  • $2.90 a ride; free after your 12th tap within 24h on the same card
  • Each person taps their own card — can't share
  • Mind Uptown (north) vs Downtown (south) — wrong way = tap out & re-enter
  • Open Google Maps before boarding for next-stop guidance
